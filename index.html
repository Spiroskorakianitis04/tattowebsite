About Us

Are you in search of a unique tattoo experience? Look no further!

We are a young and established tattoo shop in the heart of Corfu Old Town. Founded in 2017 by Mario, who has been in the business for over 20 years, we specialize in Traditional Tattoos. However, our tattoo artists have experience in every style and are happy to work with both our and your ideas.

Our residents and guest artists are internationally recognized. Thanks to their experience and professionalism, we offer quality and personalized tattoos, from minimalist detail to full-length designs. Take a look at the diverse portfolios and find an artist for your next tattoo project.

FAQ

Here are some common questions and answers

Book an Appointment

We are happy that our work has piqued your interest and we look forward to having you in our studio. You can contact us via email, through social media, give us a call, or simply step by and let's make your tattoo idea a reality.

How much will it cost?

The cost of a tattoo depends on its size, complexity, and the time required to complete it. Contact us for a personalized quote.

What should I do before the visit?

Make sure to eat well, stay hydrated, and avoid alcohol or blood-thinning medications before your appointment.

How long does it take for a tattoo to heal?

Healing time varies, but most tattoos take about 2-3 weeks to heal. Follow our aftercare instructions for the best results.

Do you do walk-in tattoos?

Yes, we accept walk-ins based on availability. However, we recommend booking an appointment to ensure your spot.
