About us

Are you in of a unique tattoo experience? Look no further!

FAQ

Here are some common questions and answers

How do I book an appointment:

we are happy that our work has piqued your interest and we look forward to having you in our studio. You can contact us via email, through social media, give us a call or simply step by and let's make your tattoo idea a reality

How much will it cost?

The price if a tattoo depends on varius factors, like the size, the placement and the complexity of the design.

What should I do before the visit?

Get a good night's sleep, Stay hydrated, Eat healthy food and bring snacks,shoer, Dress comfortable, speak about allergies or diseases, Do not party hard/take drugs or alcohol

How long does it take for a tattoo to heal?

A few days after the tattoo is complete, the skin around the tattoo starts peeling, similar to a sunburn. It is crucial to avoid picking on the paint, as this can damage the artwork and increase the risk of infection. After having a tattoo, the peeling process may end 7 to 10 days, leaving the skin shiny and slightly wrinkled. After two weeks the tattoo is fully healed.
